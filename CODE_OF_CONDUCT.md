# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in PDMCGuard a respectful and
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

**Expected behaviour:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it thoughtfully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Assume good faith — most people are trying to help

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other official communication channel.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to
**conduct@pdmcguard.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that
do not align with this code of conduct, and to temporarily or permanently
ban contributors for behaviour they deem inappropriate.
